# 📌 Project Overview

This project is an **interactive Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze social media performance across multiple platforms. It helps businesses and marketing teams monitor audience engagement, compare platform performance, and identify the most effective content types.

The dashboard transforms raw social media data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations and KPI metrics.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

✔ Analyze social media performance

✔ Compare platform-wise views

✔ Identify the best-performing content type

✔ Track engagement trends

✔ Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs)

✔ Support data-driven marketing decisions

# 📉 Key Insights

### 📺 Platform Performance

- 🥇 **YouTube** generated the highest number of views (**1.29B**).
- 📸 **Instagram** ranked second with **640M** views.
- 📰 Medium showed moderate performance.
- 💼 LinkedIn recorded the lowest reach.

---

### 🎥 Content Performance

- **Video content** contributed over **61%** of total views.
- Stories and Images delivered moderate engagement.
- Articles and Polls generated the least audience interaction.

---

### 📅 Engagement Trends

- 📈 Highest engagement occurred on **Friday (11.51%)**
- 📈 Sunday also showed strong engagement (**11.33%**)
- 📉 Wednesday had the lowest engagement rate.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

✅ Increase investment in **Video Content**

✅ Focus marketing campaigns on **YouTube**

✅ Schedule major posts on **Fridays**

✅ Improve LinkedIn content strategy

✅ Repurpose successful videos into short-form content
